CHICKEN STROGANOFF RECIPE!!

Follow along!

Chicken Stroganoff is a comforting dish featuring tender pieces of chicken sautéed with onions and mushrooms in a rich, creamy sauce made with sour cream and broth. It's served over egg noodles, rice, or mashed potatoes for a hearty and delicious meal. Perfect for family dinners and gatherings.

THIS IS WHAT YOU'LL NEED!

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken breasts or boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces.
  • Onion, finely chopped.
  • Mushrooms, sliced.
  • Butter or cooking oil.
  • Sour cream.
  • Chicken broth or stock.
  • Dijon mustard(optional, for extra flavor).
  • Worcestershire sauce(optional, for tangy kick).
  • Paprika.
  • Salt and pepper to taste.
  • Fresh persley, chopped(for garnish).
  • Egg noodles, rice, or mashed potatoes(for serving).

GETTING YOUR HANDS DIRTY!

THAT'S HOW YOU DO IT!

  1. Heat a large skillet or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add butter or cooking oil to the pan and let it melt.
  3. Add the chopped onion and sauté for 2-3 minutes until it becomes translucent.
  4. If using garlic, add minced garlic to the pan and cook for an additional 30 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Add the sliced mushrooms to the pan and cook for about 5 minutes until they are tender and lightly browned.
  6. Push the onions and mushrooms to one side of the pan, and add the chicken pieces to the other side.
  7. Cook the chicken until it's browned on all sides and cooked through (no pink in the center).
  8. In a separate bowl, mix the sour cream with chicken broth until well combined.
  9. Pour the sour cream mixture into the pan with the chicken, onions, and mushrooms.
  10. Stir in Dijon mustard and Worcestershire sauce (if using) for extra flavor.
  11. Season the mixture with paprika, salt, and pepper to taste.
  12. Reduce the heat to low and let the stroganoff simmer for a few minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  13. If the sauce seems too thick, you can add more chicken broth to achieve the desired consistency.
  14. Once the chicken stroganoff is ready, garnish with chopped fresh parsley for a pop of color and added freshness.
  15. Serve the Chicken Stroganoff over cooked egg noodles, rice, or mashed potatoes for a hearty and satisfying meal.
